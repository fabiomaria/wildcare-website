What guides us

Four Principles

Relationship > Perfection

It's not the finished work that counts, but what happens between us. How do we take up space? How do we make space?

Micro-Utopias

We create temporary, safe spaces. Here we pause the rules of the world for a moment and practice a different way of being together.

Inquiry-Based Learning

No fixed forms to imitate. We offer tools to explore your own expression and discover what moves you.

Radical Care

Not just gentleness, but also clarity. Holding your own boundaries while giving others the space to be seen.

A Lab for Living Together

What happens here?

Contact Improvisation is the playful exploration of gravity, balance, and touch. There are no fixed steps, no pressure to perform, and no assigned roles.

Through mindful physical contact, you learn to experience your body more consciously, regulate your nervous system, and communicate boundaries — in dance and in everyday life.
