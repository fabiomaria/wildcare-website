What to Expect

Contact Improvisation is the playful exploration of gravity, balance and touch. There are no fixed steps, no choreography and no pressure to perform — just you, the space and the people in it.

You learn to share and release weight, to listen through touch and to become more aware of your body. Along the way, you naturally regulate your nervous system, practise communicating boundaries and develop a fine sense for yourself and those around you.

Each session begins with a guided warm-up and flows into an open jam — a free dance time where anything can happen: stillness, movement, encounter.

“You don’t need any dance experience. Just comfortable clothing, curiosity and the willingness to try something new.”

Funded Beginners

Bewegungsrevolution

Contact Improvisation for everyone

A free 10-week programme for people with no movement experience at all. Funded by the Bewegungsrevolution initiative and ASVÖ — because access to physical practice shouldn’t be a question of money or prior experience.

Over ten progressive sessions, you explore the foundations of Contact Improvisation: body awareness, sharing weight, falling and catching, touch as communication. No prior knowledge needed — truly none.

Performance / Lecture

We Just Met / First Encounter

Making the social physics of dance visible

A format where a narrator freezes the dancers during a Contact Improvisation session — and explains to the audience what is happening on a physical and social level. Gravity, trust, boundaries: the invisible physics of encounter becomes readable.

Our attempt to make Contact Improvisation accessible to an audience that has never heard of it.

Free Contact Improvisation in Graz parks

Dance under the open sky — low-threshold, free and visible to all. Contact Improvisation out of the studio and into public space. Because movement shouldn’t be a luxury.
