Visual Identity

Living Earth,
Clear Voice.

The visual identity of Wild Care reflects our values: Organic and warm, but never vague. Clear and honest, but never cold. This document defines colours, typography, tone, and design principles.

Core Statement

We live in a world of unprecedented change. To find new ways of living together, it is not enough to think them into being — we must experience and practice them together.

From the Wild Care Manifesto

03 — Principles

Four Principles

Every design decision follows these principles — they reflect the manifesto.

Relationship > Perfection

Authentic, Not Polished

Real photos instead of stock imagery. Handwritten elements are welcome. Small imperfections make it human.

Micro-Utopias

Creating Spaces

Generous white space. Each section should feel like its own room to step into. Breathing pauses between blocks.

Explorative Learning

Invite, Don't Lecture

Questions instead of answers. “Come by” instead of “Book now”. The website should spark curiosity, not sell.

Radical Care

Clear and Accessible

Readable font sizes. High contrast. Accessible navigation. Care also shows in how we shape information.
