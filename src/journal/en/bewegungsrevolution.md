The time has come: Wild Care has received a grant of €490 through the ASVÖ and the "Bewegungsrevolution" programme. Starting 11 May 2026, we're launching a free 6-week Contact Improvisation block — open to everyone who wants to move. No prior experience needed, no membership fee, no barriers.

The course takes place every Monday from 17:45 to 19:15 at Orpheumgasse 11, Graz, running from 11 May to 15 June 2026. Over six weeks, we offer a safe space to discover Contact Improvisation from the ground up: sensing weight and gravity, finding balance with a partner, communicating through the body rather than words.

> Movement is not a luxury. It belongs to everyone. When we learn to consciously inhabit our bodies, it changes not just the dance — but how we meet each other.

Behind this project is the conviction that access to artistic body practice should not be a matter of money. With the Bewegungsrevolution grant, we can put exactly that into practice: creating a space that is truly open to all. Radical care — not as a nice word, but as concrete practice.

At the end of the 6-week block, there will be a public showing: a lecture-performance in the format *We Just Met*, where we make the artistic research process visible for an audience — the invisible social physics of encounter becomes legible.

Curious? Never danced before? Perfect. That's exactly what this project is for. Just come by — in comfortable clothes, with bare feet and a sense that it's okay to explore new territory. We look forward to seeing you.
